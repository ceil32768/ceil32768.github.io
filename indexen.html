About Me

I'm Yu-Ying Chen (Ceil), a first-year Computer Science & Information Engineering student at National Cheng Kung University, and founder of Ceilusion Studio.

Since my first release in 2021, my games have passed 100,000 downloads — all built solo, in my spare time, without a team.

The real world has too many rules. I won't be defined by my circumstances, or live inside standard answers. My dream is simple: to build one landmark game that leaves a mark.
