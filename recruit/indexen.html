Why Join?

1. Profit Sharing

This is not free labor. Although there is no fixed salary during the development phase, the project will adopt a "Net Profit Sharing Model".

  • Every revenue generated after the game is released on Steam (after deducting costs) will be distributed based on the contribution weight of members. If the game succeeds, we share the glory and wealth.
  • We are partners, not an employer-employee relationship.

2. Resume Booster

As students, we may not lack GPA, but what we lack is a real commercial product launched on the market.

When you go for an interview in the future, the value of this experience far exceeds ten classroom assignments.
