Roadmap

Ceil Studio Strategic Roadmap: Our Vision & Endgame

📍 Phase 1: Elite Selection & Preparation (Now — 2027.02.04)
"This is your ticket to a commercial hit — but you must prove you can carry it."

This phase is the team's preparation core. We will run multiple high-intensity Game Jams as live stress tests. It's a survival-of-the-fittest trial: only developers who show absolute growth in both skill and mindset will earn a core member seat and future profit share.

📍 Phase 2: Pipeline Validation & First Ship (2027.02.04 — 2027.05.04)
"No theory — we validate our edge in the market."

Surviving core members will sign the Core Member Commitment. In three months, we will start and complete our first Steam indie game from development to launch. The goal is not profit yet — it's to fully validate our commercial development workflow.

📍 Phase 3: Commercial-Grade Polish (2027.05.04 — 2027.07.01)
"From 'can make games' to 'can make hits'."

Retrospective on market feedback and team collaboration from the first game. Over two months, we will run intensive iteration to align our tech, art quality, and execution with international hit standards.

📍 Phase 4: Flagship Horror Title Launch (2027.07.01 — 2028.07.01)
"Sharpening done. Main event begins."

With the team battle-ready, we officially kick off full development of Ceil Studio's first commercial horror game. We will pour in all funding, tech, and marketing to create a landmark title that makes waves on Steam.

📍 Phase 5: IP Universe Expansion (2028.07.01 — 2030.07.01)
"Replicate hits with hits — scale the profit."

Building on the first horror game's success and reach, we directly start development of the IP's second installment. With a mature pipeline and fan base, we aim for exponential profit growth.

🚩 Phase 6: Financial Freedom & Legendary Exit (2031)
"We're not here to be wage slaves forever — we're here to flip the script."

Core members achieve financial freedom through game profit share. Ceil Studio will formally dissolve. We don't aim to be a century-old company — we aim to be the most legendary dev squad in the industry. Mission complete. Honorably retired.

Why Join

Why Join?

1. Shareholder-Level Wealth Distribution

We're not looking for free labor — we're looking for future "business partners".

  • Every revenue generated after the game is released on Steam (after deducting costs) will be distributed based on core members' contribution weight.
